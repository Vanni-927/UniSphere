Q&A

Q.

This college campus is so confusing and big, what are the best food options out there?

Ans.

There’s good healthy food at foodcourt-4, and addicting choco-lava & Ice-Tea at Nescafe. The 4H Canteen is a pocket friendly yummy option

Q.

How do I study for ECE-101 (Basic Electronics), my exams are in a week and I have zero prep.

Ans.

You should follow the channel Neso Academy on youtube and solve past year paper questions. Focus more on numericals and solve the book if you have time left

Q.

How many weeks before exam should we start our prep??

Ans.

For mid-sem examination a week or 2 is suffiecient and for end-sems 15 days approx is sufficient. But it is always advised to learn regularly to avoid workload!
